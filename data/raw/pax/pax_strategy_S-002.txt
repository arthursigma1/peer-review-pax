THREE-YEAR TARGETS (2025-2027):

FUNDRAISING:
- 2025 target: $6B (initial), raised to $6.3-6.6B mid-year
- 2026 target: $7B
- 2027 target: $8B
- Cumulative 3-year target: $21B total fundraising

FEE-RELATED EARNINGS (FRE):
- 2025 target: $200M-$225M (FRE margin 58%-60%)
- 2026 target: $225M-$245M ($1.42-$1.54/share)
- 2027 target: $260M-$290M ($1.60-$1.80/share)
- FRE margin objective: 58%-60%

AUM TARGETS:
- Total AUM target: $60B by end of 2027
- Fee-Earning AUM (FEAUM) target: $70B by end of 2027 (pro forma including acquisitions)

PERFORMANCE-RELATED EARNINGS:
- Target: $120M-$140M cumulative from Q4 2024 to end of 2027
- As of Q4 2025: $62M already realized

M&A TARGETS:
- Target: $18B of fee-paying AUM through acquisitions over the 3-year period
- Only ~10% of 2027 FRE target expected to come from acquisitions; focus on organic growth

STRATEGIC ROADMAP:
- Platform diversification across all asset classes
- GPMS expansion as key growth engine
- Credit platform buildout (organic and inorganic)
- Infrastructure continued leadership
- Geographic expansion beyond LatAm core

KEY THEME: The greater diversification of Patria's platform is paying off. Over a brief four-year period since IPO, Patria greatly expanded its regional and global investor base and distribution capabilities, and significantly diversified its investment and product platforms. A wide variety of strategies and products contributed to fundraising, most of which did not exist at IPO.
