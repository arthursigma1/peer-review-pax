BUSINESS OVERVIEW (from 20-F and corporate filings):

Patria is a global alternative asset management firm focused on the mid-market segment, specializing in resilient sectors across select regions. The firm is the leading asset manager in Latin America with a strong presence in Europe through its extensive network of General Partners relationships.

With 37 years of experience, Patria manages over $50 billion in assets under management across six asset classes: Private Equity, Private Equity Solutions (GPMS), Credit, Real Estate, Infrastructure, and Public Equities.

Main investment sectors: Agribusiness, Power & Energy, Healthcare, Logistics & Transportation, Food & Beverage, Digital & Tech Services.

Investment regions: Latin America, Europe, and US.

MISSION AND PURPOSE:
- "Crafting unique investment opportunities for our clients"
- "Building a legacy in the regions where we operate"
- Through investments, Patria seeks to "transform industries and untangle bottlenecks, generating attractive returns for its investors, while creating sustainable value for society."
- "Winning with purpose" — success means doing business the right way, with each investment serving a greater purpose.

STRATEGIC APPROACH:
The organization combines investment leaders, sector experts, company managers, and strategic relationships to "identify compelling investment opportunities accessible only to those with local proficiency."
- On-the-ground presence in local markets as key competitive advantage
- Co-investment alongside clients ("skin in the game") to ensure alignment of interests

CORE VALUES (Five Pillars):
1. Ethical — "We do the right thing."
2. Aligned to Clients' Objectives — active listening and shared investment
3. Associative and Complementary — partnership focus
4. United by Purpose — creating "sustainable and positive mark" on sectors
5. Disciplined, Criteria-driven and Hands-on — process-oriented value creation

COMPETITIVE POSITIONING:
- Leading alternative investment platform in Latin America
- 10 offices across 4 continents
- Global presence combining investment leaders, sector experts, and strategic relationships
