REGISTRATION OF ADDITIONAL SECURITIES PURSUANT TO GENERAL INSTRUCTION
 
Pursuant to General Instruction E to Form S-8, Patria Investments Limited (the “Registrant”) is filing this Registration Statement on Form S-8 (this

reference the contents of the Registrant’s registration statements on Form S-8 filed with the Commission on November 28, 2022 (Registration No. 333-
268577), February 26, 2024 (Registration No. 333-277375) and February 20, 2025 (Registration No. 333-285091).
