Date: 2026-02-11

KEY DETAILS:
- Preparing second vintage of LatAm private credit fund
- Expected to raise >$500M (per Bloomberg)
- Builds on 26-year, $12.3B credit platform in Latin America
- First fund closed May 2025 at $314M
- First fund: >70% deployed into 14 transactions as of Dec 31, 2025
- First fund gross unlevered IRR: 15.6%
- Historical track record: 210+ transactions, $3.1B deployed, 14.3% historical unlevered IRR
- Strategy: Senior secured, USD-denominated loans with robust collateral/covenants
- Market opportunity: Private credit <1% of LatAm's $2.3T corporate credit system
- LatAm corporate leverage: 2.8x vs 5.5x in US high yield
- Spreads 100-150bps wider than US HY

MANAGEMENT QUOTE:
Javier Montero (Partner, Head of Private Credit): Strategy targets "attractive yield without relying on higher leverage" in "under-levered" market with "scarce" reliable capital.
