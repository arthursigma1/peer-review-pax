Source: S-020 — WP Global Partners Acquisition Announcement
Date: 2026-02-02
URL: https://www.globenewswire.com/news-release/2026/02/02/3230029/0/en/Patria-Investments-Announces-Acquisition-of-WP-Global-Partners.html

KEY DETAILS:
- First U.S. acquisition by Patria
- Target: WP Global Partners (founded 2005)
- FEAUM: $1.8B
- Offices: New York and Chicago
- 30 employees including 10+ investment professionals
- Deployed >$6B across multiple sectors
- Focus: Lower-middle-market PE solutions (primaries, secondaries, co-investments)
- Deal structure: All-cash at 1.7% of FEAUM + performance-based earnout (payable 2029)
- Pro-forma GPMS FEAUM: >$13.3B (as of Q3 2025)
- ~40% of GPMS assets in U.S. post-deal
- Accretive to FRE and DE in year one
- Advisors: Latham & Watkins (legal), Raymond James (financial)

STRATEGIC SIGNIFICANCE:
- First direct U.S. market entry via acquisition
- Broadens GP relationships and client reach
- Addresses demand for middle-market PE exposure

MANAGEMENT QUOTES:
Marco D'Ippolito (Managing Partner): "This transaction immediately broadens the universe of GPs in our ecosystem and strengthens our product offering."
Donald Phillips (WP CEO): "Patria stood out for its entrepreneurial culture, diversified global platform and strong focus on mid-market private equity."
